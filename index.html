SPAMBOT EXTENSION

Gmail made easier

Spambot is a Google Chrome extension that brings organization to your inbox. Using advanced accessibility through a chrome extension, this product helps highlight and isolate certain emails in your inbox, using trained AI programs to find trends and to deduct which emails may be potentially unncecessary or dangerous. The extension works as a scraper, which tracks the current emails by subject and sender, checks using the AI to see if they are 'safe' or 'unsafe', and then highlights based on the decision made in that step. The AI is still in progress, but the product has a basic functionality at the time being.
